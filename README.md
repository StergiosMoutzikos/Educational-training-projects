##  Project 1 — Music History & Tradition: Corfu–Vienna Collaboration

 **Paper:** `Συνεργατικά_Εκπαιδευτικά_Περιβάλλοντα.pdf`

A full academic proposal for a **year-long cross-cultural music education program** connecting the Music School of Corfu and the Music School of Vienna. The project uses **Trello** for project management and **Flat.io** for collaborative music notation.

**Key content:**
- Presentation, SWOT analysis, and pedagogical justification of Trello and Flat.io
- 3-phase teaching scenario: musical research → learning traditional scores → joint online concert
- Student and teacher roles, assessment strategy, and support needs
- Screenshots of actual Trello boards and Flat.io scores created for the project

**Author:** Μουτζίκος Στέργιος <br>

##  Project 2 — Comparative & Pedagogical Evaluation of Code Games

 **Paper:** `Ειδικά_Θέματα_Διδακτικής_της_Πληροφορικής.pdf`

A structured comparative study evaluating **6 popular coding education platforms** — CodeCombat, Scratch, Code.org, Blockly, Tynker, and Lightbot — across **18 pedagogical and technical metrics**.

**Key content:**
- Systematic review of 5 relevant research studies
- 18-metric evaluation framework (ease of use, knowledge depth, fun factor, educator tools, extensibility, etc.)
- Scored comparison tables for all 6 tools
- Pedagogical discussion of findings and recommendations for educators
- Future research directions

**Author:** Μουτζίκος Στέργιος
